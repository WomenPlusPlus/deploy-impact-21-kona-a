## üßê  About 

Kona Connect‚Äôs legal team, together with students and expert volunteers from around the world, are developing a tool to connect people in need to 
the organisations best equipped to help them. This tool ‚Äì a legal aid chatbot ‚Äì accessible 24/7 through any smartphone device, will automatically 
guide its users, whilst simultaneously reducing aid agencies‚Äô staggering workloads, freeing up time to help more individuals.

This has been a 6 week volunteer project in collaboration with Kona-Connect and Women++. The team's goal is to develop an application that will connect Sengalese people to various NGO's and organizations that can provide assistance for specific needs.
